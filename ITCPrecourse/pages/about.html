About

Hi there! My name is Yanay Sova and im starting a new journey in my life, becoming a full-stack developer!

Coding is something that's always piqued my interest, the reward of problem-solving and the creative approach captured me immediately when I started fiddling with Javascript back in the day, and eventually what pushed me to go and learn it in a professional institution.

I'm mostly self-taught with everything I’ve been doing until today, from import and export coordinator to running a website for gaming and working in digital advertising, I experience a lot of career changes and now I'm ready for me next one.

For personal interests, I'm first and foremost a musician at heart! From music production to guitar playing I’m a studio octopus with many hats I switch more often than not, and I love it! Like coding, there is no limit to how much you know, its both exciting and humbling at the same time.
Other than that, I love traveling and seeing the world, being from a little country like Israel, the rest of the world looks even bigger than it is. Whether venturing to a new location or visiting an old and familiar one im always glad to hop on a plane and travel, which leads me to the next section of this page.
